Whats is the Greatest Debate?

This website was built to discuss the Greatest Debate, that being who is... The Greatest Football Player of All Time.

We have decided to choose four players we see as the clear greatest, these players being Pele, Diego Maradona, Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi.

To get clear indication on who is The Greatest of All Time we will look at statistics, trophies and overall influence on the game of Football whether through moments or career.

Above you will see a navigation towards About, if you would like to find out more about why this website was created please click that link!

If you would like to skip all the stats, facts and breakdown of who is the Greatest of All Time and why that is, click the Conclusion link to see!
